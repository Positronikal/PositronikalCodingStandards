### EXCEPTIONS: Contributing Forks

For repositories where Positronikal is a contributor rather than the originating or maintaining party, follow the origin maintainer's conventions without deviation. This applies to all aspects of development: formatting, tooling, commit format, hooks, workflow, and language choice. Do not introduce changes that require or imply adoption of Positronikal standards on a project that does not belong to us. If a maintainer's approach is sufficiently contrary to our standards to constitute a genuine problem, the appropriate response is a hard fork, not incremental pressure on the upstream.

### ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS

For tools that may be used to generate or process digital evidence in legal proceedings, see the supplementary [Forensic Evidence Tool Standards](./Forensic%20Evidence%20Tool%20Standards.md) document.
